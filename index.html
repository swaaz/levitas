ABOUT US 

We the team of formula Sahyadri are designing, fabricating and developing a student version of formula 1 Car i.e Sae supra. This event deals with design, manufacture, management, communication, research, and marketing .sae Supra is highly sought after by the engineering mobility industry.we are proud privileged to be one among 127 teams participating in the event across India. Our team although being amateurs in this event is having great knowledge and also the experience in vehicle fabrication and analysis. This prestigious event will be held at Buddh international circuit, Greater Noida.we being a United team Of 25 people greatly believe in 'unity is strength and teamwork is the milestone to success.' with the thirst of gaining knowledge and expertise with hard work, sincerity, and dedication we move forward in our pursuit of excellence with your blessings and well wishes.
